Exhibition Space for Artist Peter Liddle

Peter Liddle (b.1940) is a British landscape artist and sculptor. He has a large collection of works that he has produced during his long career. Currently they are displayed throughout his house and garden. The proposed exhibition space will be inserted into a barn adjacent to his cottage to better house his work for the interested public.

Natural stone, concrete, steel, brass and a variety of timbers have been employed to reflect Liddle's love of earthy materials. The dramatic wavelike roof and rippled doors are also inspired by Liddle's long fascination with the ocean and the coast of Britain.
