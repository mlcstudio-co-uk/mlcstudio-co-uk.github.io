Rising / Falling - Always Hoping

"A charming account of life on the road between two friends and Amelie, their Citroen 2CV. Craig Chamberlain has written a revealing and well observed account of a momentous journey from Europe to Mongolia. This intrepid duo attract a wealth of anecdotes from people and places en route making the book feel like an enjoyable ride in the passenger seat."

--Rory Stewart, MP, OBE

An illustrated narrative of two university friend's progress through Europe into the lesser know lands of Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and finally Mongolia as part of the 2007 Mongol Rally. The book is dedicated to `those who help along the way' and stands as testament to the great charity and support that the duo received before and during the rally. From mechanics in Doncaster to farmers on the Kazakh/Russian border, to families on the Mongolian Steppe, people were always there to help. Nevertheless, it proved a bumpy ride and at times the pair were misguided and felt forsaken. But Rising/Falling - Always Hoping was the mantra of the trip and even when things were at their worst, when they had lost all hope in the car, in everything, they persevered and made it to the finish line.
