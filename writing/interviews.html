In Interview With Dominique Perrault

Dominique Perrault spoke to Eva Guttmann and Craig Chamberlain, on behalf of the HDA, Graz, 27th April 2011. Topics of conversation included: form, void in the city, the responsibilities of producing "Grand Projets" and the possibilities of the artificial in landscapes. Projects discussed include the Bibliotèque Nationale - Paris, the Olympic Velodrome and Swimming Pool - Berlin, the Town Hall and Urban Rebuilding - Innsbruck, Pudong - Shanghai and the New Mariinsky Theatre proposal - St. Petersburg.

Includes photographs, visualisations, sketches and technical drawings.

In Interview With Jacob van Rijs, MVRDV

Jacob van Rijs spoke to Eva Guttmann and Craig Chamberlain, on behalf of the HDA, Graz, 29th January 2010. Topics of conversation included: political patronage in architecture, density and variety in the inner-city, the Dutch national character, working internationally and sustainability. Projects discussed include the Meta-City Data-Town - Book/Exhibition, the landmark Hanover expo-Pavilion and the Logroño Montecorvo Eco City - Spain.

Includes video, photographs, visualisations and diagrams
