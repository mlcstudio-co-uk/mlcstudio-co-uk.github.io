Valerio Olgiati

Valerio Olgiati spoke before students and professionals at Technical University of Graz, Austria on the 27th April 2011.

Olgiati gave an entertaining talk describing some of his best known projects - 'House for a Musician', museum for the Swiss National Parc in Zernez and his own studio and residance. He also defended his position on totality of design, non-contextual buildings and coherency of building materials.

Includes photographs of projects and models.
